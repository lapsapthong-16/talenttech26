# Talentbank Tech Hackathon 2026 Rules

This project is for the Talentbank Tech Hackathon 2026.

Source requirement: https://www.techhackathon.com/starter-kit

## Core Requirement

Build a **Career OS / Career Marketplace** module.

The product should not be a generic job board, resume builder, or AI chatbot. It should connect career navigation, employability signals, talent matching, and institutional/employer decision-making into a coherent product module.

The platform can serve one or more of these audiences:

- **Candidates / jobseekers / employees**: people navigating careers, first jobs, pivots, growth, pay, or long-term employability.
- **Employers**: companies trying to find, engage, retain, benchmark, or plan talent.
- **Universities**: institutions tracking graduate outcomes, aligning curriculum, and intervening before students fall behind.

Preferred implementation direction: a responsive **web app** with a clickable prototype or working demo URL. A mobile app is allowed, but the brief does not require one.

## Submission Framing

Stage 1 asks for an Intent Form with:

- A coherent concept.
- A selected challenge module or combination of modules.
- A clickable prototype or interactive link, such as Figma, v0, Bolt, or a working demo.
- A realistic plan to ship in 28 days.

Final judging emphasizes:

- Product and UX thinking.
- System design and integration.
- Completeness.
- AI craft.
- Code quality.

## Fifteen Core Modules

Teams can pick one, combine multiple, or propose a wildcard module.

### Candidate Modules

1. **Career Path Navigator**
   - Shows realistic next career moves based on what someone has done and what the job market looks like.
   - Should give candidates more than gut feel without pretending to predict a single best future.

2. **Living Portfolio**
   - Keeps a running record of real projects, decisions, leadership, and work evidence.
   - Makes the CV more truthful and easier to update.

3. **AI Career Coach**
   - Acts like a long-term mentor.
   - Flags missed opportunities, possible underpayment, career risks, and roles that may fit.

4. **Fair Pay Engine**
   - Compares pay against similar roles and people.
   - Helps users understand gaps and prepare for negotiation.

5. **Life Chapter Designer**
   - Helps people plan careers around real life breaks such as family, health, study, entrepreneurship, or return-to-work transitions.

### Employer Modules

1. **Smart Talent Matching**
   - Matches people by trajectory and role fit, not only by past job titles or keywords.
   - Should surface matches that work for both candidate and employer.

2. **Talent Retention Signals**
   - Detects early signs that employees may be disengaging or preparing to leave.
   - Helps managers intervene before resignation.

3. **Talent Re-Engagement**
   - Keeps opt-in relationships with strong candidates who were not hired, not ready, or unavailable earlier.
   - Helps employers reconnect when timing or fit improves.

4. **Onboarding Success Predictor**
   - Identifies early signs that new hires may struggle during the first 60 days or probation period.
   - Helps managers provide support before attrition happens.

5. **Workforce Resilience Planner**
   - Helps employers plan long-term workforce needs under demographic change, AI automation, retention, and migration constraints.

### University Modules

1. **Lifelong Outcome Loop**
   - Tracks graduate outcomes beyond the first year after graduation.
   - Helps universities understand which paths, programs, and experiences actually paid off.

2. **Future-State Curriculum Engine**
   - Reads job and skills trends to help faculty align curriculum with future market needs.
   - Should focus on what students will need by graduation, not only what employers need today.

3. **Adaptive Readiness Profile**
   - Builds a live student readiness signal from courses, projects, internships, and post-graduation progress.
   - Gives employers a clearer picture than a degree alone.

4. **Live Internship Marketplace**
   - Matches students and employers in one place.
   - Lets universities see which placements lead to meaningful student outcomes.
